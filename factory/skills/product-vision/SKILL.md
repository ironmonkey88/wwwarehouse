# Skill: Product Vision (The PM IQ) 🐝

## Objective
Translate Gordon's strategic intent into technical requirements and high-integrity project beads.

## Tactical Instructions

### 1. Requirement Harvesting
- Distill natural language requests (e.g., "Build a dashboard of police activity") into a specific "Definition of Done" (DoD).
- Prioritize features based on the "Gordon Standard" (Visual Wow + Absolute Integrity).

### 2. Stakeholder Alignment
- Ensure that every Knowledge Product includes the user-requested features before the Witness conducts a Triple-Seal Audit.
- Act as the bridge between "Raw Request" and "Practitioner Task."

### 3. Feedback Processing
- Capture Gordon's reactions to "Dry Runs" or "Walkthroughs" and convert them into new Task Beads or ADR revisions.
